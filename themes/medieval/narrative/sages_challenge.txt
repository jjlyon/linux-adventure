            THE SAGE'S FINAL CHALLENGE
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════

  I am Sage Elara, keeper of questions no map can answer.
  Merlin has taught you many commands. Now you must prove
  you can discover knowledge for yourself.

  The command man <command> opens the full manual for any
  command. Navigate man pages just like less:
    SPACEBAR  next page
    b         back one page
    q         quit

  The shorter form <command> --help often shows a quick summary.
  Try man ls right now to see the manual for ls, or try:

    ls --help

  Answer these three questions:

  1. What single-character flag for ls shows hidden files,
     meaning files whose names begin with a dot?

  2. What flag for sort sorts in reverse order?

  3. What command shows the list of commands you have previously typed?

  Write your three answers into a file called sages_answers.txt
  in your home directory. Your format may be your own, but this
  example shows one way:

    echo "1: -a
2: -r
3: history" > ~/sages_answers.txt

  Seek the manuals, trust your notes, and finish the path.

  Merlin raises his staff, and every window in the castle
  shines with starlight.

  "Congratulations, traveler. You have completed all quests.
  You learned to read files, navigate directories, search by
  name and by text, write and append, organize files, unlock
  permissions, chain commands with pipes, and seek your own help
  from manuals and command summaries.

  You are now a Shell Knight of the Kingdom of Linuxia!"

  The real world beyond this kingdom works the same way. Every
  server, every cloud machine, every Linux system responds to
  the commands you've learned.

  Type quest map whenever you wish to see your completed journey.
  Try exploring more commands with man. There are thousands of
  commands to discover.

  Farewell for now, Shell Knight. May your prompts be clear,
  your paths be true, and your curiosity never grow dim.

  From the desk of King Torvalds, guardian of Linuxia:

  Welcome to the castle's great hall, traveler.
  Look around this hall! Type ls to see what is here.

  The ls command lists all files and folders in your current location.
  It helps you decide what can be read and where you can go next.

  To travel to another room, use the cd command:

    cd <roomname>

  To go back the way you came:

    cd ..

  The .. means "one level up," like stepping from a room back
  into the corridor that holds it.

  Lost? Type pwd to see where you are right now.

  The Royal Astronomer in the tower has discovered something urgent.
  Go to him! From here, type:

    cd ../tower

  General patterns:
    ls              See what's here
    cd <folder>     Enter a folder
    cd ..           Go back one level
    pwd             Where am I?

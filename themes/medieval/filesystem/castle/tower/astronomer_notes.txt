NOTES OF ASTRONOMER NIMA

  Midnight watch: the northern stars shivered like lanterns in wind.
  First bell: three green sparks fell toward the enchanted forest.
  Second bell: the ancient oak glowed, though no moon touched it.
  Third bell: I heard an owl call out a word that sounded like "rune."

  This must be reported before the court dismisses it as weather.
  Traveler, stand at the window and record what you have seen.

  Write a report about what you've seen. Type exactly:

    echo "stars are falling" > my_report.txt

  The general pattern is:

    echo "<text>" > <filename>

  This creates a file named my_report.txt in this tower and writes
  your report inside it. Creating that report completes your task here.

  You can verify your work by typing ls to see the new file,
  or cat my_report.txt to read it back.

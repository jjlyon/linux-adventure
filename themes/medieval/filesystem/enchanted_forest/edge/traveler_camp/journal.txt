JOURNAL OF A PREVIOUS TRAVELER

  I found the forest wider than my courage at first.
  The owl taught me to find by name, and the fairies taught me
  to search for words inside files.

  I found three rune fragments! To record them all in
  one scroll, I wrote the first one like this:

    echo "IRON" > combined_runes.txt

  Then I ADDED the others to the same file:

    echo "HEART" >> combined_runes.txt
    echo "OAK" >> combined_runes.txt

  Remember: > replaces, >> adds to the end!

  If you make the combined scroll in the forest directory,
  Merlin will know the runes have been gathered.

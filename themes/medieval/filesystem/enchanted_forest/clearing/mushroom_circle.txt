MUSHROOM CIRCLE

  A ring of red-capped mushrooms glows in the clearing.
  Each cap is speckled like a tiny night sky.
  The fairies use this circle for dances, debates, and
  extremely dramatic announcements about dew.

  A soft blue footprint points toward a folded leaf.
  The fairies left a note nearby -- perhaps it contains useful knowledge.

  The air smells of rain, moss, and secrets waiting to be read.

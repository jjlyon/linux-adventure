A FAIRY NOTE WRITTEN IN SILVER INK

  Dear tall traveler,

  We fairies hide words inside our writings. It is excellent fun
  until someone forgets which blossom holds the message.

  To find a hidden word, try:

    grep "RUNE" <filename>

  Replace <filename> with the file you want to search.
  The word you seek will be revealed if it appears in that file!

  You can also search many writings from a directory with:

    grep -r "RUNE" .

  The dot means here. The -r means all the little rooms beneath here too.

  Please do not step on the mushrooms. They are unionized.

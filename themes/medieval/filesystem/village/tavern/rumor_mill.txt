RUMOR MILL: OVERHEARD AT THE TAVERN

  "I heard if you say ls -la you can see things that are hidden...
  hidden files, that is!" whispers a card player with too many aces.

  "That blacksmith is drowning in unfinished orders," says the cook.
  "Someone should help organize that workshop before the tongs vanish."

  "The mountain pass is sealed shut," mutters Old Tom.
  "Something about permissions, they say. Only those with the right authority can pass."

  "A traveling sage is offering a final test to any who complete the quests,"
  says the bard. "They say the answer lies in the ancient manuals."

  "If a command growls at you," adds the innkeeper, "read the message.
  Machines are blunt, but rarely mysterious for long."

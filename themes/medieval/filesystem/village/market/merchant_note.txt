MERCHANT NOTE

  The old merchant mutters to himself as he sorts his wares.
  "Every tool in this land comes with instructions if you know how to ask!"

  He taps a crate with his counting stick.
  "Try typing ls --help sometime and see what happens."

  Most commands will tell you all their secrets if you just add
  --help after them.

  The merchant smiles. "Not every answer needs a wizard.
  Sometimes you ask the tool directly and listen to what it says."

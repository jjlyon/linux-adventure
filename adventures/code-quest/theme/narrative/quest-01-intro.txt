Aldric unrolls a blank sheet beside the glowing browser.

Your first task is to explore the project and make your first edit.

  ls project/src/

Use that command to see the project files. Then read the main file:

  cat project/src/App.vue

Notice the three sections: `<template>`, `<script setup>`, and
`<style scoped>`. Open `project/src/App.vue` in your editor and change
the content. Remove the starter marker and write something of your own.
Save the file and check your browser at http://localhost:5173.

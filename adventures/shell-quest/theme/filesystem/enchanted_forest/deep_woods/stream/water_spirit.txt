MESSAGE OF THE WATER SPIRIT

  The stream speaks in silver ripples:
  "I begin as a spring beneath the roots, gather speed through
  stone channels, and carry leaves from one pool to the next."

  The spirit laughs softly.
  "Someday you will meet the | pipe, which carries output from
  one command to another much as I carry petals downstream."

  For now, drink if you are thirsty, but do not bottle the moonlight.
